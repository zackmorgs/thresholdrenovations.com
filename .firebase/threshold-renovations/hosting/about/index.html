About

Founded in 2023, Threshold Renovations is a locally owned business based in Winona, Minnesota, specializing in home renovations, fencing, decks, and custom outbuildings.

We take pride in delivering exceptional craftsmanship with close attention to detail. We believe that every project—whether it’s refreshing a home, building a new deck, or creating a one-of-a-kind structure—should reflect both quality and care.

At Threshold Renovations, we value transparency and fairness. We are committed to providing our clients with reliable service, honest communication, and results that stand the test of time—all at a fair price.

From concept to completion, our goal is simple: to make your vision a reality while ensuring a smooth, stress-free experience.

Meet Sam Kendall

Sam Kendall

Sam Kendall, owner and operator of Threshold Renovations, is a Rochester, MN native who has proudly called Winona home since 2010. After earning his Associate’s Degree in Carpentry from Minnesota State College–SE Technical in 2011, Sam began building his career in the local community.

Over the years, he has developed a reputation for his eye for detail, reliability, and a genuine commitment to helping clients bring their home improvement visions to life.

Sam lives in Winona with his wife, their three children, a couple lively dogs, and a flock of backyard chickens.

When he’s not on the job site, you can usually find him outdoors, working on house projects, or spending time with his family.

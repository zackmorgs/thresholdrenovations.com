About Our Services

At Threshold Renovations, we provide a full range of home improvement and construction solutions tailored to your needs. From small repairs to major upgrades, our team combines craftsmanship, reliability, and attention to detail on every project.

Whether you’re looking to enhance your home’s interior with fresh drywall and flooring, open up your living space with custom carpentry, or add value and comfort through decks, outdoor spaces, and outbuildings, we’ve got you covered.

We also specialize in windows, doors, and specialty projects, ensuring your property is both functional and inviting. No matter the size of the job, we approach it with the same commitment: to make your vision a reality.
